# Exercise 1
Let $\mathbb{Z}^2 = \mathbb{Z} \times \mathbb{Z}$ be a group where
$$\mathbb{Z}^2 = \text{\\{} (m, n) \mid m, n \in \mathbb{Z} \text{\\}}$$
Let $H = \text{\\{} (x, y) \mid x + y \geq 0 \text{\\}}$. Is $H$ subgroup of $\mathbb{Z}^2$?
